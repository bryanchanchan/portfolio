Campaign

This project is about creating a campaign for a coming soon product, in this case, I have choose the product PS5. The concept of the campaign is something that I’ve came up with which is using the idea of virtual reality to upsell this product PS5.

Since the concept of virtual reality I’ve came up with, I was I could mix some of the real life object and some surreal illustration to create feel of something going into the world where things aren’t real. I’ve included hands that are real and re-creating their hands by illustrating 3 different character's hand. Then I have also came up with a background that is something representing the virtual world.
